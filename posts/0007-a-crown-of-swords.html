A Crown of Swords

A Crown of Swords (abbreviated as aCoS by fans) is the seventh book of The Wheel of Time fantasy series written by American author Robert Jordan.

It was published by Tor Books and released on May 15, 1996. A Crown of Swords consists of a prologue and 41 chapters.
